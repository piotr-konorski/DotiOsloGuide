ABOUT ME

Let me show you Oslo!

Dorota Joanna Konorska


Hello! My name is Dorota and I have been a certified Oslo Guide since 2016. I am accredited by Oslo City Mayor Ordfører Marianne Borgen.

I am from Gdańsk and have been living in Oslo for over a decade.

I speak Polish, Norwegian, English and understand Danish, Swedish and a little bit of Spanish.

I would love to show you around Oslo:

  • we can visit the biggest tourist attractions like Fram museum, Vigelandsparken or Holmenkollen skijump, experience local authentic hipster neighbourhoods like Grünnerløkka or take a scenic guided tour along the Akerselva river where the old factories and myself will tell you all about industrialization of Norway!
  • or how about a GLAMOUR tour around the Renaissance part of Oslo with the Grand Hotel where the Nobel Peace Prize Laureate stays after receiving the Prize at Oslo City Hall every year on December 10th?
These are just some examples - I have a lot to show you in Oslo.

Every tour is individually tailored.

I am very much interested in Norwegian (and Scandinavian) History, Architecture, Art and mostly PEOPLE here!

I guarantee you will not regret choosing me.
